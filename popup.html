Syriac keyboard designed for Niles North & West Assyrian classes

How to Enable the Keyboard:

  1. Open your Chromebook Settings app.
  2. Go to Device → Keyboard → Change input settings.
  3. Under "Input methods", click Add input methods.
  4. Search for Syriac and click to add it.
  5. Click the clock area in the bottom-right corner of your shelf.
  6. Click your current keyboard language (e.g., "US") and select Syriac.
Pro Tip: Press Ctrl + Space to quickly switch back and forth between English and Syriac!
