Syriac Neo Aramaic keyboard designed for Chromebooks running ChromeOS

How to Enable the Keyboard:

  1. Open your Chromebook Settings app.
  2. Go to Device → Keyboard and inputs → Input settings.
  3. Under "Input methods", click Add input methods.
  4. Search for Syriac and click to add it.
  5. Click on the "US" icon on your taskbar.
  6. Then select Syriac. You will now be able to type with the Syriac keyboard
Pro Tip: Press Ctrl + Space to quickly switch back and forth between English and Syriac!
